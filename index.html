Space-themed booth with products mapped to spaceship metaphor. Sensory activations tied to Autism Society giveback. Dirty Soda Bar in Meeting Room 25A.
Product Stations
→Luna (Coordinates) | Entity resolution
→Meridian (Navigation) | Knowledge graph
→MCP (Docking Port) | Open protocol
→Atlas (AI Flight Crew) | Conversational AI
Sensory Activations
Busy boards, sensory tubes, kinetic sand table, fiber optic lamps, aurora light, blow-up rocketship tent, noise-cancelling headphones, astronaut helmet, LED light sticks, infinity mirror
At the Cherre booth this year, we will weave sensory elements into the activation: busy boards, kinetic sand, noise-canceling headphones. Items making a conference floor, one of the most overstimulating environments you will walk into all year, a little more navigable for the people who experience it differently.
After the conference, every sensory item from our booth will be donated to the Autism Society of San Diego to support summer programming for individuals with autism and their families.
